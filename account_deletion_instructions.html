Account Deletion Request Instructions

How to Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your Principia app account and associated data, please follow these simple steps:

  1. Open your email application
    Use your preferred email client or webmail service.
  2. Compose a new email
    Create a new email message with the following details:
    Send to:
    datascience.ml.services@gmail.com
    Subject line (required):
    Account Deletion
  3. Include your account email
    In the email body, clearly state the email address associated with your Principia account that you want to delete.
    Important: The email must contain the exact email address linked to your Principia account.
  4. Send the email
    Review your request and send the email. You will receive a confirmation once your request has been processed.

Processing Timeline

Your account deletion request will be processed within 7 business days from the time we receive your email.

Note: Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding holidays. Weekends are not counted in the processing time.

Data Retention Policy

What happens to your data:

  • Account Data: Completely deleted within 7 business days
  • Personal Information: Permanently removed from our systems
  • App Usage Data: Fully deleted
  • Backup Data: Removed from all backup systems
Complete Data Removal: No user data is kept after account deletion. Your information will be permanently removed from the Principia app and all associated systems.

Important Information

  • Irreversible Action: Account deletion cannot be undone. Please ensure you want to permanently delete your account.
  • App Access: Once deleted, you will no longer be able to access your Principia account or any associated data.
  • Email Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been successfully deleted.

This page provides account deletion instructions for the Principia app as required by Google Play Store policies.

For other inquiries, please contact us at the email address provided above.
